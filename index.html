About Us

Farhoud Ventures is a journey started in 1975 by the late Sami Farhoud, an aspiring entrepreneur and a father of two.

Sami was an aspiring businessman with dreams to relocate to the United States and launch new business ventures, however, due to his untimely death at age 35, his ventures in Lebanon were terminated and his dreams were left unfulfilled.

In 2004, freshly out of high school and eager to establish entrepreneurial endeavours from scratch, Wassim Sami Farhoud began his path as an innovative mind, defying all odds while attending his university studies, and continuously establishing one idea after the other with his latest being Farhoud Ventures, a holding company.

Farhoud Ventures has embarked on a journey that is ignited by a passion for all things industrial.

As an entrepreneurial enterprise, the group aims to become a global leader in FMCG production, fostering creativity and innovation in every venture, and driving economic growth through localized solutions. With a growing portfolio of ventures, the company continues to expand its impact across the region and beyond, contributing to self-sufficient economies and delivering tailored solutions to meet local needs.
